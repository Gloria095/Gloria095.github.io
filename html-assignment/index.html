About me!

I'm currently enrolled in Algonquin College's Interactive Media Design program. My hobbies include running at least 10k every day, taking photos, spending time with friends and family, and looking for the good in everything. I consider myself to be a reliable and amusing individual. Running allows me to clear my mind while also keeping me physically fit, whereas photography allows me to capture memories and moments. I also value my friendships and family relationships, and I always make time for them. In the future, I hope to use my education and passions to create meaningful and impactful design work.
